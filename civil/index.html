Services

Civil Construction Services

Every service backed by Truett's vertical integration, meticulous documentation, and a team that treats your project like our own.

Mass Grading & Earthwork
GPS-guided cut/fill operations achieving design grades to ±0.1 ft accuracy. Rock excavation, compaction testing, and integrated trucking for all spoil and fill.
Underground Utilities
Water, sanitary sewer, storm drain, electric duct banks, and telecom conduit from mains to service connections. HDD for road crossings and utility avoidance.
Roadway & Site Paving
VDOT-standard subgrade through asphalt surface course. Curb and gutter, ADA-compliant sidewalks, pavement marking, and heavy-duty industrial pavement.
Stormwater Management
Dry and wet detention ponds, bioretention cells, permeable pavement, and riprap channels — designed for Chesapeake Bay nutrient removal compliance.
Retaining Walls
Segmental block, MSE panel, cast-in-place concrete, and soldier pile shoring for grade transitions. Geotechnical engineer coordination on every structural wall.
E&S Compliance
DEQ-compliant erosion & sediment control from day one. Silt fence, construction entrances, sediment basins, weekly inspections, and permit close-out coordination.
