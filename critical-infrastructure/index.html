Ground Grid Testing & Mapping

A substation's ground grid is the single system standing between a fault event and a fatality. It's also the one system you can't inspect by looking at it — buried conductors corrode, connections fail, and soil conditions drift, all invisibly, while design assumptions from decades ago quietly stop being true.

Verification requires measurement. We field-test grounding systems against their design basis using IEEE Std 80 and IEEE Std 81 methodology — measuring what's actually in the ground, not what the drawings say should be. The result is an engineering-grade picture of grid resistance, current-carrying integrity, and personnel safety compliance that stands up to internal audit, regulatory review, and relay coordination studies.

Whether it's a commissioning test on new construction, a periodic assessment on an aging grid, or a post-event investigation, our crews deliver the same thing every time: defensible data, clear findings, and a prioritized path to remediation.

Testing Services

A Complete Grounding Assessment Program

Each test is performed to published IEEE methodology, witnessed and documented in the field, and reported against your design basis — not a generic template.

Soil Resistivity Surveys
Wenner four-point traverses across multiple orientations and spacings, reduced to multi-layer soil models. The foundation of every credible grid design, upgrade study, and GPR calculation.
Fall-of-Potential Testing
Ground grid resistance measured per IEEE Std 81, with full slope-method validation and lead placements engineered around site constraints — congested corridors, energized yards, and limited right-of-way included.
Grid Integrity & Continuity Testing
High-current injection point-to-point testing that finds what visual inspection can't: corroded conductors, failed connections, and isolated equipment grounds — mapped and ranked by severity.
Touch & Step Voltage Assessment
Measured touch and step potentials at fences, operating handles, equipment, and gates — evaluated against IEEE 80 tolerable body-current limits for your fault levels and clearing times.
Ground Grid Mapping & As-Builts
Non-destructive location of buried grid conductors and connections, delivered as CAD as-builts. Indispensable for stations with lost or unreliable drawings — and for planning expansions without cutting the grid.
GPR Studies & Remediation Design Support
Ground potential rise analysis, current division studies, and engineering support for grid upgrades. When testing finds a deficiency, we hand you a solution — and can mobilize the crews to build it.

Records Review & Test Plan
Drawings, fault data, prior test history, and site constraints reviewed. A station-specific test plan is issued for your approval before anyone rolls a truck.
