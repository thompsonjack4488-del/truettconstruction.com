Demolition Services

Every service backed by Truett's vertical integration, meticulous documentation, and a team that treats your project like our own.

Full Structure Demolition
Complete above and below-grade removal using tracked excavators and high-reach equipment. Engineered sequencing plan, debris sorting, and final compacted grade delivery.
Selective Interior Demo
Surgical strip-outs preserving structural elements and MEP systems to remain. Dust containment, off-hours scheduling, and clean broom-swept handoff for follow-on trades.
Hazmat Coordination
Full abatement project management: asbestos, lead, mold, PCB removal under licensed abatement contractors. EPA NESHAP compliance, air monitoring, waste manifesting.
Concrete Cutting
Diamond blade sawing, core drilling, hydraulic breaking, wire sawing, and robotic demolition. GPR scanning prior to all cuts to locate rebar and conduit.
Industrial Demolition
Decommissioning and removal of manufacturing equipment, process piping, mechanical systems, and USTs with lock-out/tag-out protocols and environmental oversight.
Material Recycling
Up to 90% material diversion through on-site sorting and licensed recycling facilities. Waste diversion documentation for LEED and sustainability reporting.
