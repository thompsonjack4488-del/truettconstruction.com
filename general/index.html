General Contracting Services

Every service backed by Truett's vertical integration, meticulous documentation, and a team that treats your project like our own.

New Commercial Construction
Ground-up office, retail, medical, and mixed-use delivery. Preconstruction budgeting, subcontractor qualification, MEP coordination, and CO management.
Tenant Improvements
Commercial fit-outs in occupied buildings — phased scheduling, dust containment, landlord MEP coordination, and off-hours work to protect adjacent tenants.
Insurance Restoration
