Land & Property Services

Every service backed by Truett's vertical integration, meticulous documentation, and a team that treats your project like our own.

Land Acquisition Support
Pre-acquisition due diligence: wetland delineation, RPA boundaries, floodplain analysis, utility availability, and preliminary yield analysis before you close.
Entitlement & Permitting
Rezoning applications, SUP/CUP filings, site plan management, and plat recordation across Fairfax, Loudoun, Prince William, Fauquier, Arlington, and Alexandria.
Environmental Compliance
Army Corps 404/401 permits, Chesapeake Bay RPA compliance, CBLAD applications, VPDES registration, and stormwater nutrient credit coordination.
Site Clearing & Grubbing
Selective clearing per approved tree save plans, mass clearing with tub grinder, invasive species removal, and timber harvest coordination for merchantable timber recovery.
Site Preparation
GPS-controlled rough grading to ±0.1 ft, cut/fill optimization, rock excavation, building pad preparation, and utility rough-in to the pad.
Development Consulting
Highest and best use analysis, pro forma cost modeling, regulatory risk assessment, off-site improvement analysis, and infrastructure cost-sharing negotiation.
