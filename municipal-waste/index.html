Services

Municipal Waste Management Services

Every service backed by Truett's vertical integration, meticulous documentation, and a team that treats your project like our own.

Landfill Cell Development
New MSW cell construction: mass earthwork, HDPE and GCL liner system installation, leachate collection, gas management infrastructure, and complete CQA documentation package.
Transfer Station Construction
Reinforced concrete tipping floors, compactor equipment pads, truck bay framing, stormwater containment, leachate collection, and full site work including scales and gatehouse.
MRF Construction
Single-stream and dual-stream materials recovery facility construction. Tipping floors, equipment foundations, sorting platform framing, bale storage, and fire suppression coordination.
Landfill Closure Systems
Engineered closure cap construction: final grading, barrier layer, drainage layer, vegetative cover, gas extraction, and post-closure drainage infrastructure.
Leachate Management
Collection ponds and lagoons with liner systems, force main conveyance, pumping stations, aeration pre-treatment, spray irrigation systems, and monitoring infrastructure.
Environmental Monitoring
Groundwater monitoring well networks, perimeter gas probes, surface water sampling stations, stormwater BMPs, and access road construction for compliance monitoring access.
