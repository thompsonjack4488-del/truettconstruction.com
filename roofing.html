What We Do

Full-Spectrum Roofing Services

From a single-family repair to a multi-building commercial installation — Truett Roofing delivers with precision, manufacturer-backed warranties, and zero shortcuts.

Residential Roofing
Full roof replacements, repairs, and new construction roofing for homes of every size. Asphalt shingle, metal, tile, and architectural systems — installed to last decades.
Commercial Roofing
TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen, and metal roofing systems for commercial and industrial buildings. Engineered for performance, longevity, and code compliance.
Roof Repair & Restoration
Leak detection, partial repairs, membrane restoration, and emergency patching. We extend the life of your existing roof with precision diagnostics and targeted solutions.
Storm Damage & Insurance
Hail, wind, and storm damage assessment with full insurance claim support. We work directly with your adjuster to ensure a fair settlement and fast restoration.
Roof Inspections
Comprehensive roof health assessments for home buyers, sellers, and property managers. Detailed reports, photos, and actionable recommendations — no pressure, no surprises.
Gutters & Drainage
Seamless gutter installation, replacement, and guard systems. Proper drainage is the last line of defense for your foundation — we do it right the first time.
