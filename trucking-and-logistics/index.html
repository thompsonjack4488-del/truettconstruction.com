Trucking & Logistics Services

Every service backed by Truett's vertical integration, meticulous documentation, and a team that treats your project like our own.

Earthwork Hauling
Tri-axle and quad-axle dump trucks dispatched to match excavator production rates. Tight cycling eliminates spoil pile buildup. GPS load tracking with daily tonnage reports.
Aggregate Delivery
Quarry coordination and delivery sequencing synchronized with paver speed. Certified weight tickets on all aggregate loads. Multiple quarry sources for redundancy on large projects.
Equipment Transport
RGN and fixed-neck lowboy transport for construction equipment, steel, and structural components. Oversize/overweight permits in all 7 states. Emergency overnight transport available.
Demolition Debris Hauling
Roll-off container service, crushed concrete to recycling facilities, steel scrap routing, and licensed C&D waste disposal with manifest tracking and waste diversion documentation.
Ready-Mix Coordination
Plant scheduling for large continuous pours, truck cycling management to match placement crew speed, on-site QC coordination, and hot-mix delivery timed to maintain mat temperature.
Fleet Dispatch Services
Standalone hauling for GCs, developers, and public agencies. USDOT-compliant drivers, unified ticketing, load reporting, and prevailing wage compliance for public projects.
