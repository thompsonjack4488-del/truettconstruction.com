Services

Waterproofing Services

Every service backed by Truett's vertical integration, meticulous documentation, and a team that treats your project like our own.

Below-Grade Waterproofing
Pre-applied HDPE/LLDPE sheets for blind-side applications, post-applied fluid membranes, crystalline systems, and drainage composite — protecting foundations from hydrostatic pressure.
Plaza & Deck Systems
Hot-applied rubberized asphalt, cold-applied liquids, and traffic-bearing coatings for occupied pedestrian decks, parking structures, and exterior walkways under load.
Commercial Roofing
TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen, and built-up roofing by manufacturer-certified crews. 15, 20, and 25-year NDL warranty programs from SOPREMA, Carlisle, and W.R. Grace.
Electronic Leak Detection
Low-voltage vector mapping and high-voltage spark testing on every installed membrane. Thermal infrared scanning for existing roof moisture mapping. ELD is standard — not an upsell.
Injection Grouting
Polyurethane foam and epoxy injection for active leaks in parking garages, elevator pits, tunnels, and below-grade walls — stopping water at the source, not the stain.
Waterproofing Remediation
Forensic investigation with core samples, ELD, and thermal scanning to identify the actual failure mode — written report before any repair scope is written.
